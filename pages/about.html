Who We Are

More Than a Game

Cricket in Sri Lanka is not just a sport — it's a national passion. From dusty village grounds to the iconic Sinhalese Sports Club, cricket connects people across all communities and generations.

Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) was founded in 1948 and became a full ICC member in 1981. Since then, the country has grown into one of the most respected cricketing nations in the world.

This website is dedicated to celebrating the achievements, legends, and spirit of Sri Lanka cricket — from the 1996 World Cup triumph to the next generation of stars.

Achievements

What We've Won

Sri Lanka's trophy cabinet speaks for itself.

🏆

ICC Cricket World Cup

1996 — Won under Arjuna Ranatunga. Defeated Australia in the final in Lahore. Sri Lanka's greatest sporting moment.

🥈

World Cup Finals

Reached the 1975, 2007, and 2011 World Cup finals, proving consistent excellence across different eras of cricket.

⚡

ICC T20 World Cup

2014 Champions — Defeated India in the final in Bangladesh with a brilliant team performance led by Lasith Malinga.

🏟️

Champions Trophy

2002 joint winners alongside India — a shared title that reflected both teams' dominance during that tournament.

📊

World Records

Home to Muralitharan's 800 Test wickets, Jayasuriya's fastest ODI 50, and multiple batting and bowling milestones.

🌟

ICC Hall of Fame

Multiple Sri Lankan legends inducted — Muralitharan, Jayasuriya, Sangakkara, Jayawardena — among cricket's immortals.
